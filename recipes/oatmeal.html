quick healthy oatmeal

ingredients:

  • 1 cup oats(50-60 grams)
  • 250 ml milk
  • a banana or dry fruits
  • bit of sugar or jaggery

thats it!!

steps to this delicious stuff:

  1. put the milk to heat
  2. add the raw oats to it
  3. let it cook for 2 minutes
  4. great!! its ready , add some dry fruits or banana slices
  5. enjoy
