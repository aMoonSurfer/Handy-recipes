Paneer rice( very tasty and healthy!!)

ingredients:

  • long rice
  • oil
  • cottage cheese
  • 2 cups water
  • spices
  • chopped veggies
  • bit of salt

warning:takes a bit long to make around 30 minutes but, believe me it's worth it!

The process:

  1. cook the rice
  2. add oil to the pan and heat
  3. lightly fry the veggies and add some spices like black pepper
  4. add some more water and add the paneer into it and cook
  5. add salt and then the cottage cheese cut into cubes
  6. cook for 5 more minutes
  7. this cool dish is ready to eat!!!
