5 minutes Protein pasta

Things that you need:

  • an instant pasta pack
  • some chopped carrots
  • cottage cheese
  • 2 cups of water

How to make in 5 minutes:

  1. cook the instant pasta with water for 2 minutes
  2. add the chopped carrots and veggies and cook a bit more
  3. add the cottage cheese
  4. ready to eat ,enjoy
